Thrilling Episodes Await

With over 1000 episodes, One Piece delivers action, humor, and emotion in every episode. Witness the Straw Hats' incredible journey!

  • Épisode 1: "Romance Dawn" - Luffy's journey begins as he sets out to become the Pirate King.
  • Épisode 44: "Alabasta Arc" - The crew faces Baroque Works and saves the kingdom of Alabasta.
  • Épisode 130: "Skypiea Arc" - The crew explores Skypiea, an island in the sky, and confronts the powerful Ener.
  • Épisode 223: "Water 7 Arc" - The crew encounters Franky and battles the CP9 to save Nico Robin.
  • Épisode 309: "Thriller Bark Arc" - The crew faces Gecko Moria and welcomes Brook to their crew.
  • Épisode 517: "Marineford War Arc" - The crew witnesses the epic war between the Marines and Whitebeard Pirates.
  • Épisode 578: "Fish-Man Island Arc" - The crew confronts Hody Jones and aids the island's residents.
  • Épisode 783: "Whole Cake Island Arc" - The crew takes on Big Mom and strives to rescue Sanji.
  • Épisode 958: "Wano Kuni Arc" - The crew battles Kaido and Orochi to liberate the land of Wano.
